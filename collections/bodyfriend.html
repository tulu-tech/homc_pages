Bodyfriend Technology Explained

What makes a Bodyfriend massage chair different from other premium brands? Here are the key technologies.

XD Pro Massage Module

Bodyfriend's proprietary massage module delivers 1.25mm precision targeting. Unlike standard 4D rollers, the XD Pro system provides finer control over pressure depth and location for a more personalized experience.

ROVO Walking Technology

Found in the Falcon SV, ROVO technology simulates a walking-like motion through the lower body. This can help promote circulation and provide a unique stretching sensation in the calves and legs.

Zero Gravity Positioning

All Bodyfriend massage chairs feature zero gravity recline, elevating your legs above heart level to reduce spinal compression and allow the massage to reach deeper into the back and shoulders.

Advanced Air Compression

Strategically placed air cells — up to 87 in the Phantom Rovo — inflate around your arms, legs, shoulders, and feet. This rhythmic compression complements the roller massage for full-body coverage.

Body Scanning & Recognition

Each session begins with sensors mapping your body's dimensions and pressure points. The Leonardo DV goes further with BIA (Bioelectrical Impedance Analysis) to read body composition before customizing programs.

Heat Therapy System

Integrated heating elements warm muscles before and during massage. The Falcon SV reaches up to 131°F, while the Phantom Rovo uses heated massage balls for a more targeted warmth application.
