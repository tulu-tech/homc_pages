Fujiiryoki Technology Explained

What makes a Fujiiryoki massage chair different from other premium brands? Here are the key Japanese-engineered technologies across the lineup.

Flex Frame SL-Track

Found in the Cyber Relax Pro, Elite, and MR-6000 Calm Plus, the Flex Frame SL-Track follows your spine's natural curvature from neck to glutes. The flexible frame also reclines fully flat, allowing deeper upper-back contact than rigid tracks.

5D AI Massage Robot

The Cyber Relax AI features a 5D system that adjusts intensity and technique in real time as your muscles respond. AI body scanning maps your body shape, shoulder position, and spinal alignment before every session for a truly personalized massage.

4D Dual Massage Robots

The Cyber Relax Pro uses two independent 4D robots — one for your back, one for your glutes — working simultaneously. This "two-therapists" feel provides coverage and depth that single-robot chairs cannot replicate.

Zero Gravity Positioning

All Fujiiryoki massage chairs feature zero gravity recline, elevating your legs above heart level to reduce spinal compression. This position allows the massage rollers to reach deeper into the back and shoulders with less resistance.

Multi-Zone Heat Therapy

Heat therapy ranges from dual-zone feet and lumbar warming (Cyber Relax AI) to a quadruple heat system covering neck, arms, legs, and lumbar (Cyber Relax Pro). The MR-6000 Calm Plus features a unique heated lumbar blanket for soothing lower-back warmth.

Advanced Calf & Foot Systems

Fujiiryoki's triple-action leg massage combines Shiatsu rollers, kneading airbags, and full-leg compression. The Cyber Relax Elite accommodates up to size 13 feet with removable insoles for adjustable intensity.
