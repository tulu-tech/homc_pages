Frequently Asked Questions

Are massage chairs worth it?

A premium massage chair is worth it for buyers who want regular at-home comfort, relaxation, and a more convenient wellness routine. The real value depends on comfort, fit, feature quality, and the ownership experience around delivery, warranty, and support.

What is the best massage chair for home use?

The best massage chair for home use depends on body fit, preferred massage feel, room compatibility, and feature priorities such as zero gravity, 3D or 4D rollers, stretching, heat, and full-body air compression.

Where can I try massage chairs near me?

Buyers in San Jose and the Bay Area can compare premium massage chairs in person at House of Massage Chairs at Westfield Oakridge Mall, where multiple brands and models can be tested side by side.

Is a zero gravity massage chair worth it?

For many buyers, zero gravity is one of the most noticeable comfort features because it creates a more reclined, floating position that can make the session feel more immersive and relaxing.

How much does a good massage chair cost?

A good massage chair can vary widely in price depending on engineering, features, brand, fit, and support. Premium buyers should evaluate total ownership value, not price alone.

Can I compare massage chairs in person in San Jose?

Yes. HOMC's San Jose showroom gives buyers the chance to compare multiple premium massage chairs and brands in one location before making a decision.

Do premium massage chairs come with delivery and warranty?

Premium purchases often include stronger ownership support. HOMC highlights white-glove delivery and setup, manufacturer-backed warranty, and in-house service as part of the buying experience.
